Each Continent

Asia shows the highest mineral production. Oceania has less minerals produced; however, it has greater mineral density considering the land area.

In Australia ...

The Top 5 minerals produced in Australia are Bauxite, Cooking Coal, Iron, Lignite, Steam Coal.

Capita income

In general, low income countries have less mineral production. High income countries don't always have the highest mineral production, but usually still produce a good amount of that mineral.

Precious Metals

Palladium

Palladium is the most valuable precious metal compared to gold, silver and platinum. It can be seen that the least developed countries produce no palladium, whereas developing and transition countries produce a larger amount of palladium which plays a great role in develoopment.

Production Ranking

The ranking of palladium production is relatively constant throughout the years. Only the first and last few rankings have small changes.
